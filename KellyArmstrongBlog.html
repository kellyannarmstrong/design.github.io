Blog Post #1: Digital Marketing Campaigns

A digital marketing campaign is a big-picture plan that uses digital strategy to achieve a company’s objective. Successful design of these campaigns can result in enhanced performance and lead generation, improved conversion rates, higher revenues, and a better brand reputation. Five commonly used digital marketing campaign types include social media marketing campaigns, content marketing campaigns, off-page CEO campaigns, email campaigns, and omnichannel marketing campaigns.

Aside from business and e-commerce, these campaigns also serve a vital role in the dissemination of public health and philanthropic messages. Creativity and a genuine understanding of the target audience of your message can make all the difference in the success of a campaign design. Authenticity can also go a long way, and is a key factor in what makes certain campaigns go “viral”. Some of the most memorable 2018 digital marketing campaigns so far range from commercials, billboards, and social media ad series.

One example of influencer marketing and powerful video content working together is Amazon’s Alexa commercial. This campaign, dubbed “Alexa Has Lost Her Voice”, featured a diverse range of world-renowned celebrities and aired at the superbowl, eventually earning a whopping 47 million views. This digital marketing campaign is a prime example of the power of creative humor and influencer marketing.
