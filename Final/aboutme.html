About Me


Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Kelly Armstrong, and I am a student at the University of California, Davis. This portfolio highlights my favorite pieces from my design career thus far, and represents various techniques and mediums, including Bristol, Crowquill, and Watercolor.

Design is one of my majors, but I am also double majoring with Communication - my desire is to harness critical design thinking and apply it to the digital marketing field. I believe that creativity is essential to effectively communicate with the world.

When I'm not designing, I love to spend time with animals, children, and my friends and family. I get most inspired when I am on long beach hikes with my three dogs in sunny Southern California.

Thank you for checking out my page, and be sure to reach out with any questions or feedback! :)
